Home – Animals and Longtermism Community

We are a community focused on improving the lives of all sentient beings in the present and future—including humans, non-human animals, and digital minds.

We have two online community hubs:

  • Hive Slack: This community hub lives on the #c-longtermism channel within the Hive Slack, a global community of over 2,000 farmed animal advocates. The channel hosts monthly community calls focused on longtermist issues in animal welfare. You can join Hive here.
  • Discord server: The Non-humans and Longtermism Discord server is the first online community dedicated to discussions of non-humans and the long-term future.
